My TOP 3 Movies Of All Time

The Godfather

The Godfather (1972) - Directed by Francis Ford Coppola,
this crime epic is based on Mario Puzo's novel and stars Marlon Brando and Al Pacino.
It explores the Corleone crime family and is praised for its
powerful performances, engaging storytelling, and iconic moments.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption(1994) - Directed by Frank Darabont and based on a Stephen King novella, this film
follows the lives of two imprisoned men played by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman. It is celebrated for its
compelling characters, emotional depth, and a captivating narrative about hope, friendship, and redemption.

Citizen Kane

Citizen Kane(1941) - Directed by Orson Welles, this groundbreaking film is often hailed as a masterpiece of cinema.
It tells the story of Charles Foster Kane, a newspaper magnate, and explores themes of power, ambition, and the
complexities of human nature. "Citizen Kane" is renowned for its innovative cinematography, narrative structure
and lasting cultural impact.
